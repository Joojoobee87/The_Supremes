Current members...

Diana Ross

Diana Ernestine Earle Ross is an American singer, actress, and record producer. Born on 26 March 1944 and raised in Detroit, Michigan

Mary Wilson

Mary Wilson is best known as the founding member and longest member of the Supremes, born in Greenville, Mississippi on 6 March 1944

Cindy Birdsong

Cynthia Ann Birdsong became a member of The Supremes in 1967, when she replaced co-founding member Florence Ballard.Born in Mount Holly, New Jersey on 15 December 1939. Cindy had previously been a member of Patti LaBelle & the Bluebelles

Former members...

Florence Ballard

Florence was the founder of the group which originally began as The Primettes, a sister groups at the time to The Primes. She was a key member of the trio up until 1967 when she was dropped from the group and replaced by Cindy Birdsong. She attempted to ignite her solo career with ABC records but unfortunately proving unsuccessful and eventually she was dropped from the label at the end of the decade.
